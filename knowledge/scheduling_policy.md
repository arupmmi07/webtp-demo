# Scheduling Policy
**Last Updated:** November 14, 2025
**Version:** 2.3

## Appointment Types

### Physical Therapy Sessions
- **Duration:** 45-60 minutes
- **Advance Booking:** Minimum 24 hours notice required
- **Cancellation:** Must cancel 24 hours in advance or $50 fee applies

### Initial Evaluation
- **Duration:** 90 minutes
- **Requirements:** 
  - Valid referral (if insurance-based)
  - Insurance pre-authorization (if applicable)
  - Photo ID and insurance card
- **Advance Booking:** Minimum 48 hours notice

### Follow-up Sessions
- **Duration:** 45 minutes
- **Frequency:** As prescribed by therapist (typically 2-3x per week)
- **Requirements:** Active treatment plan

## Scheduling Rules

### Appointment Times
- **Monday-Friday:** 7:00 AM - 7:00 PM
- **Saturday:** 8:00 AM - 2:00 PM
- **Sunday:** Closed

### Booking Windows
- **Standard patients:** Can book up to 30 days in advance
- **Existing patients:** Can book up to 60 days in advance
- **Same-day appointments:** Available if slots open (call clinic)

### Patient Limits
- Maximum 3 future appointments per patient
- No-show policy: 2 no-shows = admin review required before next booking

## Special Situations

### Workers Compensation
- Requires employer authorization BEFORE first visit
- Must provide claim number and adjuster contact
- No co-pay, billed directly to workers comp

### Auto Accident/Personal Injury
- Requires attorney or insurance claim information
- Letter of protection may be accepted
- Special billing applies

### Cash Pay Patients
- No referral needed
- Payment due at time of service
- Self-pay rates apply ($150 evaluation, $100 per session)
