# Compliance & Insurance Requirements
**Last Updated:** November 14, 2025
**Version:** 3.1

## Insurance Authorization Requirements

### Medicare
- **Prior Authorization:** NOT required for outpatient PT
- **Referral:** Physician referral required (can be verbal, confirmed within 30 days)
- **Documentation:** Evaluation and plan of care within 30 days
- **Frequency:** Re-evaluation every 10 visits or 30 days

### Medicare Advantage Plans
- **Prior Authorization:** REQUIRED for most plans
- **Referral:** Required from PCP
- **Authorization Timeline:** Must be obtained BEFORE first visit
- **Valid Period:** Typically 60-90 days or specific visit count

### Commercial Insurance (Aetna, UHC, Cigna, BCBS)
- **Prior Authorization:** Required for most plans (check eligibility first)
- **Referral Requirements:** Varies by plan
  - HMO: PCP referral REQUIRED
  - PPO: Usually NOT required
  - EPO: Usually NOT required
- **Authorization Timeline:** 3-10 business days typical
- **Valid Period:** Usually 30-60 days or 6-12 visits

### Workers Compensation
- **Prior Authorization:** ALWAYS required from employer/carrier
- **Referral:** From treating physician
- **Claim Number:** MUST have active claim number
- **Documentation:** Employer must approve treatment before scheduling

## Compliance Rules

### HIPAA Requirements
- Cannot discuss appointment details via unsecured channels
- Patient must provide verbal or written consent for communication preferences
- Cannot leave detailed voicemails without consent

### Documentation Requirements
- Photo ID required at first visit
- Insurance cards (front and back) scanned
- Emergency contact required
- Consent forms signed before treatment

### Medical Necessity
- Treatment must be medically necessary
- Progress must be documented every visit
- If no progress after 3-4 weeks, re-evaluation required
- Cannot treat without physician referral (unless cash pay)

## Red Flags - DO NOT SCHEDULE

### Cannot Schedule If:
1. No active workers comp claim (for work injury)
2. No prior authorization for Medicare Advantage (will be denied)
3. Patient has outstanding balance >$500 (admin approval needed)
4. No referral for HMO patient
5. Patient is under 18 without parental consent

### Requires Admin Review Before Scheduling:
1. Auto accident claims without insurance info
2. International insurance
3. Out-of-network requests
4. Patients with 2+ no-shows in past 90 days
